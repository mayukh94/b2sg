Building a Global Startup Ecosystem for Bitsians

Vision

To serve as a common platform for BITSian entrepreneurs, mentors, investors and other stakeholders in the start-up ecosystem to connect and help each other in every possible way anytime, anywhere.

Mission

Catch-them-young, Guide-them-right and Make-them-big.

Goals

To serve as a common platform for BITSian entrepreneurs, mentors & investors.

HOW IT WORKS

Every city has a BITSIAN Startup City Hub where we meet once a month to network with like-minded people who are starting out in their city to form a cohesive local community of BITSian entrepreneurs. The city chapters further would be connected to the National Hub and the Global Hub forming a big community boosting start-up ecosystem worldwide.

It will enable BITSians in seeking help for all kinds of challenges faced while starting up from BITSIAN's globally.It would build a bridge between entrepreneurs, investors and potential resources.

It will serve as a platform to connect to BITSians in this space globally and locally in each of the individual chapters.

It will keep everyone apprised about the updates in BITSian startup ecosystem, be it the latest news, events and activities.
